Kimchi Chige

Description

Ingredients

  • Aged Kimchi
  • skinless pork belly
  • firm tofu
  • mushrooms

Steps to Cook

  1. Marinate 180g of skinless pork belly with 1 Tbsp rice wine and 3 sprinkles of ground black pepper for about 15 minutes.
  2. Cook 3/4 cup of aged kimchi in a skillet until soft.
  3. Put the marinated meat into the bottom of a pot. Add all the other ingredients (kimchi, onion, mushrooms, tofu, water, and the base sauce) except for green onion into the pot.
  4. Boil the pot on medium-high heat initially then reduce the heat to medium once it starts boiling. Cook further until the meat is cooked.
